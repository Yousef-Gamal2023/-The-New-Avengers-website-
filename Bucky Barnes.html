Bucky Barnes (The Winter Soldier)

James Buchanan "Bucky" Barnes was a brave soldier and best friend of Steve Rogers during World War II. Presumed dead in battle, he was actually captured by HYDRA, brainwashed, and transformed into the deadly assassin known as the Winter Soldier.

For decades, Bucky carried out covert missions while under mind control, leaving a legacy of destruction. But with the help of Steve and others, he began to regain fragments of his memory and humanity. His internal struggle between his past sins and his true identity became central to his arc in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

After the fall of HYDRA and the events of *Captain America: Civil War*, Bucky sought peace and healing in Wakanda, where he was deprogrammed and referred to as the "White Wolf." He later returned to join the battle against Thanos.

In the aftermath of *Avengers: Endgame*, Bucky worked alongside Sam Wilson in *The Falcon and the Winter Soldier* series to find his purpose in a new world.
