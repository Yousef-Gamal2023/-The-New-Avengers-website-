Who is Yelena Belova?

Yelena Belova was introduced as part of the Red Room, a secret Soviet program that trained female spies to become deadly assassins. She was portrayed as the younger sister of Natasha Romanoff (Black Widow).

Over time, Yelena evolved from a mind-controlled operative into a powerful independent character, seeking her own identity beyond her past. Her journey has taken her from spy missions to battles alongside the Avengers.

She's known for her courage, sarcasm, and emotional complexity, making her one of the most interesting and human characters in the Marvel Universe today.
